About

Built on genetic algorithms, not guesswork

Prometheus uses the same evolutionary principles found in nature. Each bot's strategy is a chromosome — 24 genes that encode how much weight to give each market indicator, and when to act on it.

Thousands of strategies compete. The ones that make money survive and reproduce. The losers are discarded. After 200 generations, what remains is a strategy that's been battle-tested against years of real market data.

Every Sunday, the process runs again on fresh data. Markets change — your bots adapt.
